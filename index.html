About FusionHack

Where Innovation Meets Impact

What is FusionHack?

FusionHack is a premier hackathon organized by the IEEE Students Branch at Government Engineering College, Bharuch. We're challenging brilliant minds to develop AI-powered solutions that can revolutionize traffic safety through real-time accident detection from CCTV footage.

Our Mission

To harness the power of artificial intelligence and computer vision to create life-saving technology. Your innovation could reduce emergency response times and save countless lives on our roads.

Who Can Participate?

Teams of up to 4 members passionate about AI, machine learning, and making a real-world impact. Whether you're a student, developer, or researcher, if you have the skills and drive, you're welcome!

The Challenge

Build the Future of Traffic Safety

AI-Powered Traffic Accident Detection

Build an AI system that automatically detects traffic accidents from CCTV footage within seconds. Your model will analyze 10-second video clips from road intersections and alert authorities to crashes, collisions, and pile-ups - enabling faster emergency response and saving lives.

Key Requirements:

  • ‚úì Analyze 10-second video clips from road intersection CCTV cameras
  • ‚úì Detect various types of accidents: crashes, collisions, pile-ups
  • ‚úì Provide real-time or near-real-time detection (within seconds)
  • ‚úì Generate alerts suitable for emergency response systems
  • ‚úì Minimize false positives while maintaining high accuracy

Suggested Technologies:

Computer Vision Deep Learning YOLO/R-CNN TensorFlow PyTorch OpenCV
